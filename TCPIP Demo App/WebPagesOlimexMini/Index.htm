Welcome to PIC-MINI-WEB development board!

PIC-MICRO-WEB development board is used to demonstrate the power and abilities of an 8-bit embedded web server. This board is produced by Olimex Ltd. 

The bottom portion of this page illustrates real-time control of the demo board.
